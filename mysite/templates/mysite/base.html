Welcome to PEC

The College of Engineering Pathanapuram was established in the year 2011 by the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE), an undertaking of the Government of Kerala. The College is located at Elikkatoor in Kollam district of Kerala. It is situated at a serene ambience in a vast area of twenty five acres, which is 7 Kms from Punalur and 8 Kms from Pathanapuram.
